Dine-in/take-out prices may vary. Single-use items, such as plasticware, chopsticks, condiments, and napkins, will not be included unless requested.

Distance Charge

We happily deliver to surrounding areas! The $4.99 delivery fee covers the first 4 miles from the restaurant. A $1.25 pro-rated, taxable, distance fee will automatically apply for each additional mile. Your driver receives the entire delivery fee and tip.
